Who We Are

With the blessing of Bishop Rt. Rev. Dr. Malayil Sabu Koshy Cherian, the CSI Counselling Centre offers holistic healing through compassionate, confidential, and evidence-based counselling. Our services are guided by a commitment to mental, emotional, and spiritual well-being.

Our Services

Individual Counselling

Personal & online sessions for stress, anxiety, grief, and related challenges.

 Couple & Family Therapy

Resolve conflicts and strengthen family relationships with professional guidance.

 Child & Adolescent Therapy

Support for behavioral, academic, emotional development, and career guidance.

 Psychodiagnostics

Assessments for learning difficulties, psychological testing, and career planning.

 Premarital Counselling

Three-day residential program to prepare couples for a strong and faithful marriage.

 School & College Outreach Programmes S.H.A.P.E. - Student’s Holistic and Pragmatic Empowerment

Empowering individuals to ensure holistic wellbeing through counselling and other initiatives.

 Pre-confirmational Counselling

Pre-confirmational counselling is a certified half/one day programme, typically to guidance and educate individuals preferably youths who are preparing for their confirmation.
